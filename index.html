About Us

Hi,

Aisspace Donates for Girls and Orphanage for Boys were started in January 2012. We started with 5 children in 2012. Today we have 25 girls and 35 boys. The Children’s Homes are spread across 3 floors. The children are orphans, rescued street children found by volunteers, or come from very poor families or have single parents who cannot give them proper education and food. Our children call it their home. Indeed we work hard to make it so. After they have lost so much, they deserve all the security and love any normal home provides Orphanage has 2 desktop computers and 1 laptop. The children are given training in computer skills by a volunteer faculty. The staff includes 2 wardens, 3 caretakers and 2 cooks.
